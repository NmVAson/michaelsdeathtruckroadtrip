This is the official, unflinching, semi-reliable record of **Michael's Death Truck Road Trip** &mdash;
a journey undertaken by two people in a vehicle that, frankly, has no business going anywhere.

There's Michael. There's me. There's the truck.
The truck is held together by zip ties, optimism, and a profound misunderstanding of how cooling systems work.
Michael insists it'll make it. I'm documenting.

## What you'll find here

- **Episodes.** Short video dispatches from the road. Filed when there's signal.
- **A map.** So you can see exactly where it broke down.
- **An RSS feed.** For the seven of you who still use one.

## Why

Because if I don't write it down, no one will believe me.

*&mdash; The Passenger*
