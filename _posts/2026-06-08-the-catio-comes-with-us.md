Michael has built one-fifth of a catio.

A catio, for those of you who have somehow avoided the discourse, is a patio
for a cat. The remaining four-fifths exist as a sketch, a stack of cedar, and
some chicken wire.

For roughly ninety seconds, the plan was to bring the unfinished one-fifth on
the trip. He was going to finish it on the road. 

&ldquo;eh, nevermind.&rdquo;

The catio remains.

> The cat was not consulted at any point in this process.
